Service and Maintenance

Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing accounts for a significant amount of the maintenance required for a structure. The trained engineers at Global Central AC Works SP L.L.C will work to ensure all your existing systems are in proper working order by developing a full understanding of your specific property and needs. The services offered are designed to ensure that both preventive and corrective measures are taken at the appropriate time.

Electrical System

Our electrical system services encompass a wide range of solutions, from installation to maintenance of electrical systems in commercial and residential buildings. We ensure that all electrical components are functioning efficiently and comply with safety standards. Our team is equipped to handle everything from simple repairs to complex installations, providing you with reliable and safe electrical systems.

Plumbing & Fire Fighting System

We offer comprehensive plumbing services, including installation, repair, and maintenance of plumbing systems. Our fire fighting systems are designed to provide maximum safety and efficiency, ensuring that your property is well-protected against fire hazards. Our team of experts is trained to install and maintain these systems to the highest standards, giving you peace of mind and security.

Mechanical System

Our mechanical system services include the design, installation, and maintenance of HVAC (Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning) systems. We provide customized solutions to meet the specific needs of your property, ensuring optimal performance and energy efficiency. Our experienced technicians are dedicated to delivering high-quality service, ensuring your mechanical systems operate smoothly and effectively.
